Peru

Peru is a fascinating country located in South America. It's well know for it's rich Inca heritage and breathtaking landscapes. The country lies between Ecuador to the north and Chile to the south. It's capital city, Lima, is the political and economic center of the nation, where most government offices are located. Peru offers a unique mix of ancient culture and modern life, making it an unforgettable destination for travelers.
